Essential Elements of a Resume

Your resume is often the first impression you make on potential employers. Make sure it includes the following:

  • Contact Information: Ensure your details are up-to-date.
  • Professional Summary: A brief overview of your career goals and qualifications.
  • Work Experience: Detail your relevant work history with achievements.
  • Education: List your academic background and any certifications.
